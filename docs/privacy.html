Privacy Policy — DTrain

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

DTrain ("the app") is an AI-powered triathlon training app for iPhone. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Data We Access

DTrain may read the following data from Apple Health on your device, with your explicit permission:

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV) — used to calculate your daily readiness score
  • Resting Heart Rate — used to assess cardiovascular fitness trends
  • Sleep Analysis — used to evaluate recovery quality
  • VO2 Max — used to estimate aerobic fitness level
  • Completed Workouts — used to compare prescribed vs. actual training

DTrain may also write the following data to Apple Health:

  • Workouts — training sessions you log through the app

How Your Data Is Used

All health data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate personalized daily workout plans
  • Calculate your readiness and recovery scores
  • Power the on-device AI coach's responses and recommendations

Data Storage

All data stays on your device. DTrain does not operate a backend server or cloud database. Your health data, athlete profile, workout history, and coach conversations are stored locally using Apple's secure on-device storage. No data is transmitted to any external server.

Third-Party Sharing

DTrain does not sell, share, or transmit your personal or health data to any third party. There are no advertising SDKs or analytics services embedded in the app.

On-Device AI

The AI coach feature runs entirely on your device using a local language model. Your conversations with the coach are never sent to an external server.

Apple Health Permissions

DTrain requests access to Apple Health data only after you grant explicit permission through iOS's standard permission prompt. You can revoke this permission at any time in:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → DTrain

Children's Privacy

DTrain is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
